Tax Information

Tax Rates and Exemption Requirements:

  • Uline is required to charge tax in all U.S. States and the District of Columbia, except DE, MT, NH and OR.
  • Uline requires exemption certificates from all customers claiming a tax exemption.
  • Tax rates are generally determined by the shipping address of an order.

Create New Tax Exemption Certificate:

Create Tax Exemption is not currently available.
Please submit tax exemption certificates via upload, email or fax.

Exemption certificates will be processed within 1 business day.

Instructions:

  • Create a tax exemption or
    upload an existing tax exemption, and place your order.
  • Tax on your order will be adjusted based on your certificate if processed before invoicing.
  • Most certificates are processed same day, but they may take up to one business day.
  • If your invoice contains tax, please contact our Tax Department and we will issue a credit.

Document Requirements:
