Pickup / Will Call Orders

To place a pickup order:

  1. Select a shipping address nearest the branch you wish to pickup from.
  2. Specify "Pickup/Will Call" on the next page.
  3. Follow the prompts to specify a pickup date and time.

Please allow at least one hour between the time you place your order and pickup. Uline does not offer retail store locations for browsing products.

Orders not picked up within 7 days of scheduled date will be canceled.
