RFC # Format

Business/Company
  • 12 characters in length (no dashes)
  • The first 3 characters represent the business name. It could be different based on what the SAT has available.
  • Example: Uline Shipping Supplies S de RL de CV = USS
  • The next 6 characters represent the company’s registration date (YYMMDD)
  • Example: 800130
  • The last 3 characters are assigned randomly by the tax authority
  • Example: MH7
  • The complete RFC number of the example above is USS800130MH7
Individual
  • 13 characters in length (no dashes)
  • The first 4 characters represent the individual’s full name
  • First 2 letters of the First surname, First letter of the Second surname and First letter of the first name (in case there are 2)
  • Example: Pedro GOnzalez Acosta = GOAP
  • The next 6 characters represent the individual’s date of birth (YYMMDD)
  • Example: 850217
  • The last 3 characters are assigned randomly by the tax authority and are alphanumeric
  • Example: TE7
  • The complete RFC number of the example above is GOAP850217TE7
